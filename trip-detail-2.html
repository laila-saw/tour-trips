3 DAYS DESERT TRIP FROM FES TO MARRAKECH

3 Days Desert Trip from Fes to Marrakech, Presented to you by Prime Morocco Tours.

What are Fez day trips?

Marrakech day trips are excursions that visit interesting places near the Red City. Marrakech is situated in a very good geographical spot that allows its visitors to enjoy many wonderful places, while they’re still booking in a hotel there. These day trips usually start early in the morning by leaving Marrakech, and they finish before it gets very dark. The day trips are a very good opportunity to discover the natural landscapes of Morocco without travelling for long periods of time.

The day trips from Marrakech usually visit Essaouira, which is a coastal city with an ancient history. Throughout history, this wonderful place has attracted many civilizations and cultures; the buildings and the archaeological findings are there to prove how rich this place is. It’s also a very artistic city; musicians, painters and traditional artisans consider Essaouira a holy land. Essaouira is also a very famous stop for surfers.

Some day trips visit nearby waterfalls like  Ouzoud and Ourika. The waterfall excursions are a great opportunity to discover the hidden beauty of the Moroccan natural landscapes and the biodiversity; Ouzoud waterfalls are home to macaque monkeys.

Other day trips visit the Hollywood of Morocco, which is Ouarzazate and Ait Ben Haddo combined. The two places are very popular in film culture; you can see these two spots in movies like Gladiator, Kingdom Of Heaven, and Lawrence of Arabia, even the very famous series Game of Thrones has some scenes in these studios. The area is very famous with its wonderful old architecture that you can see in the Kasbah and Ksours.

There are also Marrakech day trips that visit the high Atlas Mountains area, where the highest peak in North Africa is situated; it’s called Mount Toubkal. The day trips usually visit Imlil village, which is a valley village. The region is very peaceful and calm, which makes it the best stop for people who are tired of the busy lifestyle of the Red City. This excursion is a great opportunity to try some traditional Moroccan cuisine.

Marrakech day trips itinerary.

Fez day trips itinerary The day trips leave Fez early in the morning. Depending on the trip that you might choose, the schedule might be different; the activities that each place offers play a huge role in determining what’s going to happen: usually taking a guided tour, visiting a market or a shop, having a meal and having private time to explore individually each place. The day trips usually end before dark.

The highlights of Fez day trips

  • The historical journey to the Roman ruins of Volubilis.
  • The visit to the landmarks of Meknes city.
  • The visit to the cedar forests and the fun time with the monkeys.
  • The exploration of Moroccan culture and history in Moulay Idriss.
  • The fun time in Blue City Chefchaouen with its Spanish style.
